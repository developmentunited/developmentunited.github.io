OUR STORY

Development United started as a research project within the department of Global Health and Social Medicine at King’s College London. It was spearheaded by a group of passionate students who realized that the system of development assistance we currently exist in is highly inefficient. Their research revealed that one of the largest reasons that this system is so inefficient is because collaboration within the field of international development is often not feasible or ineffective. To address this problem, Development United was formed.
